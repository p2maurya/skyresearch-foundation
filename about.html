About Gudsky Research Foundation

A non-profit organization committed to bridging the gap between academia and industry through research-driven learning in emerging technologies.

Our Journey: From Concept to Foundation
2022
The Beginning
Started as a college research group, focusing on innovative projects and guiding fellow students towards research-based work.
2024
Official Registration
Officially registered as a Section 8 Non-Profit Organization, solidifying our mission and expanding our operations.
Today
A Growing Ecosystem
A thriving community dedicated to providing hands-on experience in AI, Robotics, and other emerging technologies.

Our Mission

  • Empower students with hands-on research experience.
  • Support the writing and publishing of impactful research papers.
  • Provide expert mentorship from industry leaders and PhD scholars.

Our Vision

To become a global hub for research and innovation. By 2028, we aim to train over 1,000 students, support 100+ publications, and partner with 50+ institutions to build a vibrant ecosystem that empowers the next generation of scientists.

The Leadership Driving Our Vision
The Founder’s Journey Abhishek Kumar Shukla
"My first exposure to the world of research came through exceptional mentors who encouraged me to explore beyond textbooks. A visit to DRDO-RCI was the turning point. Gudsky was born from these experiences—desire to create a platform where every student has the mentorship and opportunity to turn their curiosity into tangible innovation."
