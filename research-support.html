PROJECT GUIDANCE AND SUPPORT

B.Tech / UG Level

Topic & Domain Selection

Innovative, futuristic topics aligned with current industry trends. Complete feasibility analysis included.

Technology Training

Comprehensive hands-on workshops on programming, version control (Git), and Agile methodology.

Implementation Guidance

System architecture design, industry-standard coding practices, and rigorous testing support.

THESIS/DISSERTATION GUIDANCE

M.Tech / PhD Level

Research Topic & Gap

Comprehensive Scopus literature survey to formulate novel, defensible problem statements.

Proposal & Synopsis

Development of clear research objectives, robust methodology design, and milestone planning.

Systematic Literature Review

Critical analysis, synthesis of existing work, and comprehensive gap documentation.

Algorithm Optimization

Experimental design assistance, algorithm development, and statistical hypothesis testing.

Reviewer Response

Detailed analysis of reviewer comments, rebuttal letter crafting, and strategic revision support.

Thesis Writing

Chapter-wise writing guidance, professional proofreading, and Viva-Voce presentation design.

FACULTY AND PROFESSIONAL RESEARCH SUPPORT

High-Impact Publication

Targeting SCI/ESCI/Scopus Q1/Q2 journals, manuscript development, and submission management.

Grant Proposal Drafting

Identifying funding (DST, SERB) and writing comprehensive proposals with budget planning.

Profile Management

ORCID/Scopus ID configuration, h-index enhancement strategies, and Top 2% Scientist guidance.
